Welcome to TaxMaster! Here we will brief you on the working of our website.

1: Register

Register for an account to explore the functionalities.

2 : Login

Login if you are already a registered user.

3: OTP Verification

Give the OTP which you will recieve in your registered email-id.

4: Role Selection

In this page, you have to select the role,like if you have license as a tax-consultant and want to enroll yourself as a tax-advisor then you have to select the role as tax-advisor and you can help users or else you can select the role as user, calculate tax based on your income and also you can get help from tax-advisors.

5: Tax-Advisor Page

As a tax-advisor, you can calculate your tax here,you can see your appointments with users and accept and reject their requests accordingly.

6: individual Users Page

As a user, you can calculate your tax here,you can request appointments with your desired tax-consultants.
